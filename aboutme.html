A little about my story...

Renata Hoffmann

Full Stack Web Development

I am an aspiring web development student driven by a strong desire to learn and grow in the field. With each project I build, I focus on strengthening my understanding of core concepts, refining my problem-solving skills, and improving my ability to create functional applications. While I have developed a few projects throughout my course, I see every challenge as an opportunity to deepen my knowledge and enhance my practical experience. What I need now is an opportunity to continue learning skills in real-world scenarios, and expand my foundation to progress further as a developer.

Years ago, I earned a degree in Psychology, driven by a curiosity about human behavior and problem-solving. After graduation, I explored different fields—game design, interior design, and architecture—seeking the right path. Life led me to new responsibilities, including moving to Germany and focusing on family, which limited my ability to pursue further studies at the time. However, my determination to grow professionally never faded. Now, through a Full Stack Web Development bootcamp, I’ve found the opportunity to shift my career towards a field that challenges me intellectually and allows continuous learning. I’m eager grow as a developer.
